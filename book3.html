    Attitude is Everything is a self-help and motivational book that emphasizes the power of a positive mindset in achieving success. Jeff Keller, a former lawyer turned motivational speaker, shares his personal journey of transformation and provides practical lessons on how changing one's attitude can lead to significant life improvements.
