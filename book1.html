    • Overview

    This book is a comprehensive guide to the principles, processes, and applications of modern manufacturing technology. It is widely used as a textbook in mechanical, industrial, and manufacturing engineering courses. The book covers both the fundamental and advanced aspects of manufacturing, making it an essential resource for students, engineers, and professionals in the field.
