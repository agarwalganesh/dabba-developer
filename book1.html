    • Index pages (Detailed Summary)

    This book provides an extensive understanding of manufacturing processes, material properties, machining techniques, and modern automation methods. Below is a detailed chapter summary:

    • Introduction to Manufacturing: Importance, materials, and process overview.
    • Properties of Materials: Physical, mechanical properties, and testing methods.
    • Metal Casting Processes: Fundamentals, methods, and defect analysis.
    • Forming and Shaping Processes: Rolling, forging, sheet metal forming, and drawing.
    • Machining Processes: Milling, turning, drilling, grinding, and cutting tools.
    • Joining and Assembly: Welding, brazing, adhesive bonding, and fasteners.
    • Advanced Manufacturing: 3D printing, microfabrication, and non-traditional machining.
    • Automation and Quality Control: CNC, robotics, lean manufacturing, and SPC.
    • Manufacturing Systems and Design: Flexible systems, DFMA, and sustainable manufacturing.
